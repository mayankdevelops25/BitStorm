HUNDRU WATERFALL:
Into the Heart of Jharkhand

Hundru Waterfall, located near Ranchi, is formed by the Subarnarekha River dropping from 98 meters. Known for its rocky terrain and lush greenery, it is a favorite destination for trekkers and photographers.

The Roar of Jharkhand

PANCHGHAGH-FALLS:
The Scenic Escape

Panchghagh Falls, located near Khunti in Jharkhand, is formed by the breaking of the Swarnarekha River into five swift streams. Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky landscapes, it offers a peaceful and scenic escape for visitors. It is a popular spot for picnics, photography, and nature lovers.

BHATINDA-FALLS:
The Scenic Escape

Bhatinda Falls in Jharkhand is a scenic waterfall located about 14 km from Dhanbad railway station.It cascades over large rocky boulders, surrounded by lush green hillocks, making it a popular picnic spot.

HIRNI-FALLS:
Beautiful source

Hirni Waterfall, located about 70 km from Ranchi in Jharkhand, is a picturesque fall surrounded by dense forests and rolling hills. The water flows gently over wide rock formations, creating a peaceful and scenic atmosphere. It is a popular spot for nature lovers, photography, and weekend outings.
